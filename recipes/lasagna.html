Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that layers wide, flat pasta with a rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and a blend of cheeses. This hearty and comforting meal is perfect for family gatherings or special occasions. The combination of savory flavors and textures makes it a favorite for many.

Whether you are a seasoned cook or a beginner, this lasagna recipe is easy to follow and sure to impress.

Ingredients

  1. Lasagna noodles
  2. Ground beef
  3. Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Brown ground beef in a skillet over medium heat.
