Spaghetti Recipe

Description

Classic Italian spaghetti with a rich tomato sauce is a timeless favorite. This simple yet delicious dish combines al dente pasta with a flavorful sauce that's sure to please everyone at the dinner table.

This recipe is perfect for both weeknight dinners and casual entertaining.

Ingredients

  1. Spaghetti pasta
  2. Ground beef
  3. Tomato sauce
  4. Italian herbs

Steps

  1. Boil water and cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Brown ground beef in a large pan.
  3. Add tomato sauce and herbs, simmer for 20 minutes.
